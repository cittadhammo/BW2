--- What's New? ---

Early 2023: 

The option to turn Dark Mode on and off was added.

Feb 2023:
The option to turn Pali Lookup on and off was added


Keyboard shortcuts added for Next sutta, Previous sutta, Home Page, Contact Page, Help Page, turn showing Pali on and off, navigating to the next higher level of navigation and finally, to turn Pali Lookup on and off.

Ajahn Brahmali's translations of the Vinaya are replacing the older ones based on I B Horner's translations. The Khandakas are the first to be updated with most of them now completed.

April 2023:
All the requested keyboard shortcuts have been implemented. A checkbox has been added to the bottom of each page so that Pali lookup can be turned on and off on devices which don't have a keyboard.
Redundant navigation has been removed
The Study Guide now has it's own top level menu item 
Ajahn Thanissaro's Buddhist Monastic Code (BMC) has been removed from the site so that the content is more accurately "the word of the Buddha" rather than comtemporary teachers. Copies of the BMC in .mobi. epub and pdf formats can be downloaded free from here: https://tinyurl.com/ykrx8fkc

May 26th 2023
The English Search function was wrongly indexed but is now fixed.
